Frequently Asked Questions


Do we offer home deliveries

Groci store, does offer home deliveries for oreders confirmed before 4pm.
Orders confirmed past that are delivered the next day.


Is there a minimum order for delivery

Yes, for delivery orders.
This requirement helps us manage
the logistics and ensure efficient service.
The minimum order amount may vary based on your location,
so please check the details during the ordering process.


Can I track my order

Yes, once your order is confirmed and dispatched, you may be able
to track the progress of your delivery. We provide order tracking
information, which includes estimated delivery time.


How do I place my order

To place an order, visit our online grocery store website.
Browse the available products, select the items you want, and add
them to your virtual shopping cart. Proceed to the checkout,
provide the required information, and confirm your order.


What if a product is out of stock

As Groci store, serving our customers is our top priority, allowing stock
to run out is a rare occurrence but in such scenarios we update on our web page
